bio

Hi! I am Larissa Carvalho, a Graphic Designer and Illustrator.

I am from Brazil and I am currently living in Toronto. I am an Architect who found a passion in Illustrations and Graphic Design. I am enthusiastic and creative. I love learning new things and I am always searching for ways to improve my skills. I believe that there is always a story to be told through Graphic Design and Illustrations. In my home country, I have worked as a volunteer designer and I have also done freelance work. I am excited to continue my Graphic Designer career in Toronto.
