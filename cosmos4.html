COSMOS Font Design

About the project

Cosmos is a modern style uppercase typeface inspired by the stars. Its thin strokes make it minimalistic. It was hand drawn digitally with a watercolor texture. It also has ornaments on its serifs, tails, descenders and ears which makes it more magical and elegant. Another caracteristic of this typeface is its low crossbar and big counter. It also has strokes that are similar to paint strokes making the typeface more artistic.Cosmos is a decorative typeface that is perfect for headlines, titles larger sizes of text. It is not suitable for body copy. It is best for posters, branding, logos and advertisement.

Download the Cosmos Font (BETA) here and try it out!

Brief

Create my own custom typeface with an entire alphabet in all caps while maintaining the consitensy of the characters and use the principle of type design.
