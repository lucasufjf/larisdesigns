Independent Study Project

ANYORA is an Independent Project for the conclusion of the Graphic Design for Print and Web course at Humber College. ANYORA is a fictional Theme Park. I chose to work on this as my passsion project as I believe theme park is a place where the unimaginable comes to live and everything & anything is possible. The goal of this project was to Design 3 deliverables (Style Guide, Park Map and Park App) with a Fantasy theme and lots of illustrations. Embark with me on the journey of how ANYORA came to life.

Brief
To create a self-directed passion project that showcases my skills and creativity. To emphasize my project management skills and creative process. To document all my process and create a process book.

Skills

For these Designs I had to use my skills on a diversity of programs, such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe InDesign and Procreate App.
