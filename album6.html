Singularity - Album Release Package

Singularity is a fiction album, so as Alex and Lisa. This album represents through Alex and Lisa’s music the uniqueness that can be found is each of us and the things that surround us. It is an album about losing yourself and finding beauty inside. It is about dreams, fears and knowing that your sorrows and joy are all part of you. The album is also about recognizing that there are two sides in each of us, as said by the philosopher Carl Jung, we do not have to be perfect and we should overcome and accept that we all have darkness and light inside of us.

Brief

To design a complete album release marketing package that includes: Artist branding, Album Art, Printed Press Kit, merchandise and promotional Micro-site.

Skills

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, VsCode (HTML and css), Adobe Illustrator and Procreate App.
