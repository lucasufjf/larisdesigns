Tea Box Packaging

For this project I had to design the branding and the packaging for a my own imagined Tea company. Heritage Tea is a fictional organic tea company with special tea flavours, they have been on the market over 5 years and their goal is to provide the best quality. For this tea, the flavour is a raspberry hibiscus sweet tea called tea♥mo which means I Love You in Portuguese.

Brief

To design a package, logo, create a name, along with a flavour for the tea.

Skills

Adobe Illustrator and Procreate App.

By Larissa Carvalho
